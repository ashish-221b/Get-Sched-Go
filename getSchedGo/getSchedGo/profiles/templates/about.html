How to use it?

Now it's time for live action. Just click on the specific questions and boom!! You will get to know how to access each and every cool features of the application.

How to sign up???

The first time you try to access the application it will prompt you to sign up or sign in. You just click on the sign up button. It will redirect you to a form. Just fill in the details and that's it.

How to add an event??

Now that you have signed in , you will see the events tab. Just hover on it and click on the add events button. It will redirect you to a form, fill in the details and submit. The scheduler will schedule the event based on your prioities and presence of other events. Case may occur that the event is not scheduled. It will show an error message in that case else it will rredirect you the eventlist page.

How to make a profilie?

Just click on the Profile Tab in the Home Page. Fill in your preferences and timing. Check the "Are you an instructor" checkbox if you are an instructor.These will help to schedule your events in future.

How do I see all the events?

Click on the Events tab on the home page navbar. Select the EventList menu from the dropdown. You will be redirected to a page containing all the events.You can sort the events by date and id from the "Sort by DATE" and "Sort by ID" menu from the navbar. You can also click on the "Filers" menu and see only the scheduled events, only the unscheduled events or the completed events.

I want a table view of all the events, what should I do?

You must be opening the application keeping in mind a table full of events like a calender. Well here it is. Click on the "Events" tab and select the "Timetable" menu from the dropdown. Here is what you want. But you will only be able to see the scheduled events.

How to edit or delete an event?

Click on the "Events" tab, and click the "EventList" menu from the dropdown.You will see the EventList. To the right of the EventDetails, click on the trash-shaker glyphicon.The event will be deleted.

How to become an Instructor?

Just click on the Profile tab. You will be redirected to a page. Just check the "Are you an Instructor" checkbox and that's all.

As an Instructor how will you select a course?

Click on the "Courses" tab, and click the "Select Course" menu from the dropdown.You will see a form with one field prompting you to specify a course code. Just do that and submit. Ohhh!!! You have become an Instructor. Take a bow, guys!!

How to add assignments, classes, and exams as an instructor?

Click on the "Create Student Event" tab and select "Add Assignment","Add Class" or "Add Exam" as per your wish. You will be redirected to a form , just fill in the details and submit.

As a student how to enroll for a course?

Click on the "Courses" tab and select "Enroll for Course" from the dropdown.You will be redirectted to a form with just one field. Just type the course code and submit. Job done.

As a student how to see all the events related to the course?

Click on the "Courses" tab and select the "My Course" from the dropdown.Now you will be redirected to a page containing all your assignments, classes and courses. Select the "Course" tab from the navbar and select any specific course if you want to see events related to a specific course. You can also select the "Event Type" tab and select "Assignment","Classes" or "Exam" from the dropdown if you want to see only the assignment,Classes or only exams.

How to add an assignment, class or exam to your schedule?

Click on the "Courses" tab and select the "My Courses" menu from the dropdown. You will be redirected to a page containing list of course events. To the right of the course details you will see a edit-shaker glyphicon. Click on it and you will be redirected to a page containing a form. The deatails already provided will be prefilled. Add something or edit the previous as you like. Submit the form, your event will be added to the schedule if possible.In case of exam there will be an xtra calender glyphicon. You can create an ExamPreparation Event by clicking on it. Like the previous case, you will be redirected to a page containing the form. Submit it and add an ExamPreparation event to the schedule.

How to see the statistics of a given day?

Click on the "Statistics" tab.Now choose "Today" or "Yesterday" from the dropdown list.You will be shown the stats of the corresponding day.
